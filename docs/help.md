## Common Topics

**How do I add an RSS feed?**
Open the Sources section in the app, tap the “+” button, and paste your RSS feed URL. Reader will validate and subscribe to it immediately.

**Can I organize feeds into folders?**
Yes. After adding a source, you can assign it to an existing folder or create a new one. Folders appear as filter chips at the top of your inbox.

**How do I save an article to my Library?**
Tap the bookmark icon on any article, or swipe up in the Swipe Reader. The article is instantly added to your personal Library.

**How do highlights work?**
Open any article, long-press on text to select a passage, and tap "Highlight". Your highlight is saved to the article and can be shared as a styled card.

**How do I control notifications?**
Go to Settings → Notifications and set your preferred daily cadence (1–10 times per day).
