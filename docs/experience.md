# Product Experience & Design Language

Reader is designed as a momentum-based reading companion. Every screen, every transition, and every interaction is crafted to keep you in a state of comfortable focus.

## The Aesthetic

Reader's visual identity is built on warmth and restraint. The bespoke background tone (`#FFFCF5`) evokes paper without mimicking it. The accent palette — warm oranges, muted Mist Blue, and natural Sage — adds character without noise. Combined with generous whitespace and a carefully tuned typographic scale, the result is an interface that feels expensive without being showy.

Dark mode is treated as a first-class experience, not an afterthought.

## Motion & Micro-Animations

Reader uses fluid micro-animations throughout:

- **Page transitions** slide and fade with eased curves that feel natural
- **Card interactions** respond with subtle scale and shadow shifts on tap
- **The swipe reader** has spring-physics that mimic the feel of physical cards
- **Opacity pulses** on loading states keep the interface feeling alive, never frozen

Animations are never gratuitous. Each one communicates state or confirms action.

## Experience Principles

- **Fast capture, slow reading** — Getting an article into Reader should be instant. Reading it should be unhurried.
- **Minimal interruption** — During a reading session, nothing competes for attention.
- **Clear states everywhere** — Every UI element communicates exactly what it is and what it does.
- **Low cognitive overhead** — The number of decisions required per screen is always minimized.

## Typography

Reader uses clean, modern typefaces (Inter and Outfit) with a typographic scale optimized for comfortable long-form reading. Line height, letter spacing, and font size are all tuned for sustained reading sessions, not skimming.
