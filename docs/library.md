# Personal Library & Highlights

The Library is where your reading becomes your knowledge.

## What the Library Is

Every article you choose to keep is saved to your personal Library with a single tap — whether you save it from the inbox, mid-swipe in the Swipe Reader, or after finishing a full read. The Library is yours: no algorithmic curation, no expiry, no clutter. Just the articles you decided were worth keeping.

## Highlight & Share

Reader is not just a passive reading surface. While reading any article, you can select and highlight any text passage. Highlights are saved directly to the article in your Library, building a personal record of the ideas that resonated with you.

Beyond personal notes, highlights can be shared as **styled highlight cards** — beautifully formatted visuals you can post to social media or send via messaging apps. Your insights, packaged in a format worthy of the content.

## Your Library Over Time

Unlike a simple bookmark list, the Reader Library is designed to remain useful long after you save something:

- **Read state clarity** — articles are clearly marked as read or unread
- **Highlights surface key passages** without re-reading the full text
- **Search-ready** — your saved content is easily findable when you need it

Over time, the Library grows into a curated personal knowledge base, not a pile of forgotten URL bookmarks.

## Never Lose an Insight

This is Reader's promise for the Library: if you saved it, you can find it. If you highlighted it, it is preserved and shareable. The act of reading is no longer ephemeral.
