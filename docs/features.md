# Feature Set

ngpocket brings together the essential capabilities of a modern read-later app, shaped by a focus-first philosophy.

## Feature Pillars

### Capture

- Save articles from web links and feeds
- Maintain a clean intake of future reads

### Organize

- Structured saved collection
- Read-state awareness and content lifecycle clarity

### Read

- Comfortable long-form reading surface
- Clear content hierarchy and progress awareness

### Remember

- Highlights and tagging for knowledge retention
- Personal archive that grows with your thinking

### Personalize

- Reader controls tailored to individual preference
- Notification and sync behavior based on personal thresholds

### Trust

- Offline-first behavior
- Reliable continuity under variable networks

## Positioning

This is not a social feed product. It is a focused personal reading tool.
