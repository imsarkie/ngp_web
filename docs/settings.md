# Smart Notifications & Personalization

Reader respects your attention even when it is asking for it. Every personalization option is designed to support your focus, not to overwhelm you with configuration.

## Smart Notification Cadence

Instead of sending a push notification every time a new article arrives — which would be chaotic for users following many RSS sources — Reader lets you define a **daily notification cadence**.

You simply choose how often you want to be notified about new content: anywhere from **1 time a day** up to **10 times a day**. Reader handles the rest, batching and delivering curated digests on your schedule.

**The result:** No badge anxiety. No notification fatigue. Just a calm, predictable nudge that it is time to read.

## Reading Preferences

The reader surface can be fine-tuned to match your personal comfort:

- **Font size** — Adjust for your preferred reading scale
- **Theme** — Light mode (warm paper tones) or Dark mode (deep, low-strain backgrounds), both tuned as first-class experiences
- **Reading speed** — Adjust text density and layout for skimming versus deep reading

## Sync Behavior

Reader's sync is quiet by design. Your reading state, saved articles, and highlights are kept consistent across your sessions without requiring manual action. The app does the work; you do the reading.

## Sensible Defaults

Reader ships with thoughtful defaults. You do not need to configure anything to have a great experience out of the box. Personalization is offered as a path to deeper comfort — not as a barrier to just getting started.
