# Privacy Policy

This website presents the ngpocket product vision and feature identity. It does not require account creation.

## Website Data Handling

- No account sign-up is required.
- No hidden trackers are intentionally used in this website.
- Essential browser behavior may still apply for page rendering and hosting infrastructure.

## Product Data Principles

ngpocket is designed local-first. Saved articles, highlights, tags, and feed data are primarily stored on-device to preserve reliability and reading continuity.
