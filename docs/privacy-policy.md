# Privacy Policy

This documentation website explains ngpocket capabilities and does not require account creation.

## Website Data Handling

- No account sign-up is required.
- No hidden trackers are intentionally used in this documentation-focused site.
- Essential browser behavior may still apply for page rendering and hosting infrastructure.

## Product Data Model (App Context)

The ngpocket product is designed local-first, where saved articles, highlights, tags, and feed data are primarily stored on-device for reliability and offline reading.
